Sponsorship

As a nonprofit organization, Team 4533 relies on corporate sponsorships to support all of our operations. Along with covering robot and operational costs, donations support our travel expenses and competition costs as well.

4533 sponsors are featured on all team materials, such as on our robot and our team designed t-shirts. We welcome all kinds of support, ranging from in-kind to monetary!

The sponsorship levels are:

  • $100+ WALL-E: Warm glow of satisfaction, thank you note from students, WAR sponsorship decal, name/logo on website, open invitation to attend WAR build sessions
  • $500+ BB-8: All of the above and name/logo displayed in pit during competitions and 2 invites to the WAR banquet
  • $1000+ Johnny 5: all of the above & name/logo on T-shirts
  • $1500+ DATA: all of the above & name/logo on robot & and shoutouts on social media during competitions
  • $2500+ Optimus Prime: all of the above & name/logo in large font on t-shirts and robot & 2 t-shirts

Contact Giselle Cheeseman for inquiries involving sponsorships. Every dollar is appreaciated!
